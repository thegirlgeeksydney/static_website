Girl Geek Dinner Sydney

Girl Geek Sydney is a volunteer run community group focusing on promoting and engaging women in technology in Sydney. We are part of the larger Girl Geek Dinners worldwide initiative.

About Girl Geek Sydney

Girl Geek Sydney is part of a network of initiatives
that focus on promoting and engaging women in technology in Sydney.

Girl Geek Dinners

The Sydney chapter of Girl Geek Dinners, a networking group for women in IT.

Women Who Code

The Sydney chapter of Women Who Code, a group focussed on hands on coding workshops and study groups.

She Hacks

All women hackathon events. SheHacks2016 is open! Also check out last year's live tweet recap, photo album, and awards video from the May 2015 event.

Girl Geek Sydney Manifesto

  • Girl Geeks Sydney is a community offering support, networking and mentorship to women involved in technology and the web
  • Girl Geeks Sydney believe that women do not always receive fair or equal opportunities and are dedicated to changing that situation
  • Girl Geeks Sydney do not discriminate on the basis of gender, sexuality, age, race, religion or programming language
  • Girl Geeks Sydney encourage women to be fiercely, passionately outspoken
    but are committed to helping the voices of the quiet, the shy and the n00b to be heard
  • Girl Geeks Sydney believe that well behaved women seldom make history^
  • ^Laurel Thatcher Ulrich
